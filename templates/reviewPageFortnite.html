Fornite is a battle royale style game where you compete with other players to get first place. This game is very versatile, as you can play with groups of four, three, and two, with many different events going on frequently. At it's core, Fornite is a third person shooter with the added aspect of building, which makes for interesting firefights. Fortnite is a fun game if you want to compete with your friends.
